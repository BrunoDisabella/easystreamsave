Video DownloadHelper alternative

A clean Video DownloadHelper alternative for Chrome users.

Easy Stream Save is not trying to copy every legacy downloader feature. It focuses on a cleaner Chrome-first workflow: detect available web videos, preview what was found, and explain download limits clearly.

Best fit

Users who want a lightweight Chrome video downloader extension with previews, simple labels, and fewer distractions.

Not a fit

Users expecting universal downloads, DRM bypass, paid streaming access, or guaranteed conversion of every stream into MP4.
