Video download limits

Why some videos cannot be downloaded

Browser video downloaders can detect many direct media files and stream playlists, but they cannot honestly save everything on the web. This page exists to set the right expectation before you install any Chrome video downloader extension.

DRM and encrypted streams

Some platforms use DRM or encrypted media extensions to protect playback. Easy Stream Save does not bypass these protections.

Platform restrictions

Some services block downloads through their terms, technical delivery systems, or browser policies.

Temporary links

Media URLs can expire quickly, require cookies, or work only inside the original session.

What the extension focuses on

The goal is to detect available MP4, WebM, M3U8, and MPD resources clearly, then explain limits when a video is protected or unavailable.

FAQ

Why does a Chrome video downloader not detect every video?

Some players hide media behind blob playback, encrypted streams, login checks, or short-lived signed URLs. If Chrome cannot access a stable media resource, a downloader extension may not be able to save it.

Does Easy Stream Save bypass DRM?

No. Easy Stream Save does not bypass DRM, encryption, access controls, or private platform restrictions.

What about M3U8 and HLS streams?

Easy Stream Save can detect supported HLS/M3U8 playlists when the page exposes them to Chrome. Encrypted playlists, complex live streams, or protected streams can still fail.
