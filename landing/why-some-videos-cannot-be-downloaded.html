Video download limits

Why some videos cannot be downloaded

Browser video downloaders can detect many direct media files and stream playlists, but they cannot honestly save everything on the web.

DRM and encrypted streams

Some platforms use DRM or encrypted media extensions to protect playback. Easy Stream Save does not bypass these protections.

Platform restrictions

Some services block downloads through their terms, technical delivery systems, or browser policies.

Temporary links

Media URLs can expire quickly, require cookies, or work only inside the original session.

What the extension focuses on

The goal is to detect available MP4, WebM, M3U8, and MPD resources clearly, then explain limits when a video is protected or unavailable.
