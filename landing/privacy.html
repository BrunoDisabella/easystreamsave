Privacy Policy

Easy Stream Save is being built to detect downloadable media resources on the current page while keeping data collection minimal.

Data collection

The extension MVP is designed to store detected media locally in your browser. It does not require account registration for basic use.

Page access

The extension may need access to network requests or the active tab to detect media files such as MP4, WebM, M3U8, or MPD resources.

Analytics

If website analytics are added later, they should be privacy-conscious and documented here before launch.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact easystreamsave@gmail.com.
